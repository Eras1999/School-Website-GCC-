About Us

Galahitiyawa Central College is a school with a rich history and a glorious present. Since 1944, the school has managed to reach high stands nationally and Internationally, playing great roles in the fields of education, sports and many other school activities, achieving the goals of today. Galahitiyawa Central College has bestowed numerous distinguished personalities to the nation for the past years. She has fulfilled her duties in the past, and is ready to do the same in the future, even if she has to face challenges the students of Galahitiyawa Central College will go beyond them.

Vision


To be the pioneer college in the island that exalts the distinguished citizens with the aptitudes of achieving challenges globally.


Mission



To form the outstanding citizens with the potentiallity to achieve the prospective challenges equipped with the competencies in the spheres of knowledge relevant to the goals,skills, and full of adoration and benevolence for themselves and nature.
