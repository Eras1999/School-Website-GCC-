SPORTS






Where Talent Meets Opportunity




Peerless in its prowess in playing the game, the Galahitiyawa central College is acclaimed as a dominant presence in a plethora of sports. Proudly bearing numerous titles both in the island and across the seas, being a sportsman at this esteemed institution is a privilege greatly sought after. Inheriting a legacy spanning several decades, it is only natural that sports at the College have given rise to a rich culture of traditions and values. This is personified by the ethos of the men in blue and gold as they pursue not only victory itself, but doing justice to the spirit that led them to triumph. Fundamentally, the experience of being a sportsman at the Galahitiyawa central College is one like no other.
Having been tempered by relentless competition the Sportsmen of the College have attained both the confidence and skill to bring glory to their College no matter the trials that lie in their path.
